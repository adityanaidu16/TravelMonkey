Getting Started Guide

To get started in your search for attractions:
1.) Click "Get Started" on home page or "Filter" on upper right-hand corner
2.) Fill in desired filters--leave empty to show all attractions
     Note: Filters are case-insensitive
3.) Click "Submit" to view list of attractions that fit criteria and information

No results found for my desired filters?

TravelTexas aims to highlight only the best attractions and interests in Texas. As we are extremely selective, you may have to expand your filters to find a thorough and complete list of potential options. As we grow, we will be exceedingly active in adding a more diverse assortment of places to visit!

What is the score rating?

Our score ratings are derived from Google Reviews, which has thousands of testimonials and give an accurate depication of how past visitors felt about their experience.

Where can I find additional information or book/reserve an attraction?

Additional information and bookings cannot currently be found within TravelTexas, however, by simply searching the attraction via search engine, you can find the official websites. We advise that you ensure the web address is secure and starts with "https://www..." for a safe booking experience and accurate, up-to-date information.

Coronavirus (COVID-19) information for experiences

Be sure to double-check with local guidelines to ensure a smooth travel experience, as well as ensure the availabilty of specific attractions by checking official websites.
